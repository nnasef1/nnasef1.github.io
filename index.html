Hands-on approach

A simple, repeatable engagement

Whether the engagement is six weeks or six months, the shape is the same — and it's designed to leave your team stronger than I found it.

01

Diagnose

First two weeks. I sit with the program — the people, the artefacts, the room — and surface what's actually slowing it down. You get a one-page read and a sequenced plan.

02

Stabilise

Next month. We install the operating cadence the program needs to be honest with itself — clear owners, clean status, decisions made on the rhythm they should be made on.

03

Accelerate

We move from steady to fast. Cross-team blockers cleared. Hiring sharpened. Vendor accountability set. The team starts trusting the plan because the plan starts being true.

04

Sustain

The final phase is the most important. We hand the operating system back to the leaders. Coaching continues at a lighter cadence so the gains stick — and so the next thing gets shipped without me.
