From strategy to delivery

From strategy to delivery, I help teams ship the work that matters.

For more than two decades I've led mission-critical software and cloud programs — the kind that span multiple teams, vendors and time zones, and that absolutely cannot slip. I bring the structure, calm and operating cadence to move work from a stuck plan into something that actually ships.

Alongside delivery, I coach the engineering leaders carrying the weight — directors, VPs and founders navigating scale, change and the human side of building software at the edge.
